4. Reading Passage 1Read Aloud & Understand

📖 Read Aloud!
My Pet 🐾
🐱🏠🌳
I have a cat.
My cat's name is Milo.
Milo is small and white.
Milo can jump high.
Milo likes to eat and sleep.
I love my cat! 🐾
🗣️

How to read: Read the text slowly together with the students. Words printed in blue are the key vocabulary. Ask students to repeat each sentence after the teacher reads it.

❓

8. Assessment & Teaching TipsFor Teachers

⭐

Oral Reading Assessment: Score students on fluency (4 pts), pronunciation (3 pts), and confidence (3 pts). Total = 10 points per student.

🎮

Fun Activity: Create picture flash cards using the vocabulary above. Show the picture and ask students to say the English word. Award a star sticker for every correct answer!

📝

Homework: Ask students to draw their pet (or favourite animal) and write 2 sentences in English. Example: "I have a cat. My cat is black."
