Listening Script

Audio Text — Read Aloud or Play Recording  ·  2 Scripts

🎙️ SCRIPT A — "My Classroom"
Good morning, everyone! Welcome to my classroom! 🏫

This is my school bag. It is blue and red. 🎒
Inside my bag, I have a book, a pencil, an eraser, and a ruler. ✏️📏

I sit on a chair at my desk. My friend sits next to me. 🪑
My teacher is Miss Dewi. She is very kind. 👩‍🏫

Every morning, we say "Good morning, Miss Dewi!" together.
When I want to ask a question, I raise my hand. 🙋
When the teacher is talking, we are quiet. 🤫

After class, I go to the library to read books. 📖
At break time, I go to the canteen to eat a snack. 🍱
Then I play with my friends on the playground. 🏃

I love my school. It is fun every day! 😊
💡 Teacher's Tip Read the script expressively and point to real classroom objects as you mention them. Read it 2–3 times. First: students just listen. Second: students point to objects in the room. Third: students repeat each sentence.
🎙️ SCRIPT B — "Tono's School Bag" (Short Version)
Hi! My name is Tono. Look at my school bag! 🎒

I have three books — English, Math, and Science. 📚
I have two pencils and one eraser. ✏️
I also have a red ruler and a box of crayons. 🖍️

My classroom is number 1A. 🚪
My teacher is Mr. Joko. He is very funny! 👨‍🏫

I like school very much! 😄
💡 Teacher's Tip Script B is shorter and great for review or dictation. Ask students to draw each item they hear (book, pencil, eraser, ruler, crayons) on a piece of paper.
📋 ACTIVITY — Put in Order!

After listening to Script A, put the activities in the correct order!

  • 1👋 Say "Good morning, teacher!"
  • 2🪑 Sit on the chair at the desk
  • 3🙋 Raise hand to ask a question
  • 4🤫 Be quiet when teacher talks
  • 5📖 Go to the library to read
  • 6🍱 Eat a snack at the canteen
  • 7🏃 Play with friends on the playground
💡 Teacher's Tip Write each activity on a separate card and shuffle them. After listening to the script, students arrange the cards in the correct order. This builds sequencing skills while reinforcing listening comprehension.

Listening Dialogue

Everyday Conversations  ·  2 Dialogues

💬 DIALOGUE 1 — "What's In Your Bag?"
👦
Budi: Hi Sari! What's in your bag?
👧
Sari: I have a book, a pencil, and an eraser. What about you?
👦
Budi: I have a book, a ruler, and crayons. 🖍️
👧
Sari: Wow, nice crayons! Can I borrow one?
👦
Budi: Sure! Here you are. 😊
👧
Sari: Thank you, Budi!
💡 Teacher's Tip Read the dialogue with students — teacher as Budi, students as Sari, then swap roles. Bring real school items so students can act out "borrowing" while speaking.
💬 DIALOGUE 2 — "In the Classroom"
👩‍🏫
Teacher: Good morning, class!
👧
Students: Good morning, Miss Dewi! 👋
👩‍🏫
Teacher: Please open your books to page five.
👧
Sari: Yes, Miss Dewi.
👩‍🏫
Teacher: Sari, may I see your pencil, please?
👧
Sari: Yes, here it is. 😊
👩‍🏫
Teacher: Thank you! Now, let's start the lesson. 📖
💡 Teacher's Tip Practice classroom commands (open your book, raise your hand, be quiet) with actions. After reading, do a role-play where one student becomes the teacher and gives commands to friends.

Listening Exercises

Listening Practice  ·  3 Exercise Types

📝 EXERCISE A — Choose the Correct Answer

Listen to "My Classroom", then choose the correct answer! 👇

1. What color is the school bag?
2. What is in the school bag?
3. Who is the teacher's name?
4. What do students do when they want to ask a question?
5. Where does the student go to read books?
6. Where does the student eat a snack at break time?
💡 Teacher's Tip Re-read Script A before students answer. Give about 30 seconds per question. Review answers together — clap for every correct answer! 🎉
✅ EXERCISE B — True or False?

Circle TRUE or FALSE based on Tono's story (Script B)!

1. Tono has three books.
2. Tono has one pencil.
3. Tono's classroom is number 1A.
4. Tono's teacher is Miss Dewi.
5. Tono has a red ruler.
6. Tono has a box of crayons.

Listening Song

My School Song  ·  Sing & Act!

🎶 "AT MY SCHOOL" — Sing Along!
🎒 I pack my bag with a book,
A pencil and an eraser, look! 🔍
I bring my ruler and crayons too,
I'm ready for school, how about you? 😄

🏫 I walk into my classroom door,
I sit on my chair and sit some more, 🪑
I say "Good morning, teacher, hi!"
And smile up at the sunny sky! ☀️

🙋 When I have something to say,
I raise my hand the friendly way! ✋
When teacher talks, I'm quiet as can be,
Listening carefully, that's me! 👂

📖 At break time I go to the library,
I read a book so happily! 😊
Then to the canteen for a snack,
Yummy food, I'll be right back! 🍱

🏃 On the playground I run and play,
With all my friends every single day! 🤝
I love my school, it's so much fun,
Learning new things from sun to sun! 🌟

♩ Sung to a cheerful, bouncy beat ♩
Add actions for each activity while singing!
🕺 SONG ACTIONS GUIDE

Do these actions while singing the song!

  • 🎒Pack my bag — Pretend to put items into a bag on your shoulder
  • ✏️Pencil & eraser — Pretend to write, then rub it out
  • 🚪Walk into classroom — Walk in place, then mime opening a door
  • 🪑Sit on chair — Pretend to sit down
  • 🙋Raise my hand — Raise one hand high
  • 🤫Be quiet — Put finger on lips
  • 📖Library / read — Open both palms flat like opening a book
  • 🏃Playground / run — Run in place with a big smile
💡 Teacher's Tip Sing this song at the start or end of the lesson as a warm-up or cool-down. Repeat at least 3 times so students can memorize it. Award a star sticker to the most expressive student! ⭐ Teachers can also record the song for students to listen to as homework.
🗣️ SPEAKING CHALLENGE — Tell About Your School!

After listening and singing, it's your turn to talk! Use these sentence starters:

  • 1In my bag, I have a _____.
  • 2My teacher's name is _____.
  • 3My favorite school subject is _____.
  • 4At break time, I like to _____.
  • 5My best friend at school is _____.
💡 Teacher's Tip Ask each student to come to the front and share about their school using the sentence starters above. This is the output of the full listening cycle: vocab → script → dialogue → exercise → song → speaking!
