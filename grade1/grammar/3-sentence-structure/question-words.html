📖 Introduction to Question Words Starter

What are Question Words?

Question words help us ask for specific information. Instead of just asking "Yes or No?", we use question words to ask "What? Where? Who? When? Why? How?"

The Basic Question Words

What
For things - What do you like?
Who
For people - Who is your friend?
Where
For places - Where do you live?
When
For time - When do you eat lunch?

Fun Fact! 🌟

Question words are sometimes called "WH-words" because most of them start with the letters W and H! Like: What, Who, Where, When, Why, and How.

🎯 The Six Question Words Structure

Six Special Question Words

These six words help us ask different types of questions. Each one asks about something different!

1. WHAT - For Things 📦

Use "What" to ask about things, objects, or ideas.

Examples: What do you like? What is this? What do you eat?

2. WHO - For People 👥

Use "Who" to ask about people.

Examples: Who is your friend? Who plays soccer? Who is that?

3. WHERE - For Places 📍

Use "Where" to ask about location or place.

Examples: Where do you live? Where is the school? Where are you?

4. WHEN - For Time ⏰

Use "When" to ask about time.

Examples: When do you eat lunch? When is the party? When do you go to school?

5. WHY - For Reasons 🤔

Use "Why" to ask for reasons or explanations.

Examples: Why do you like soccer? Why are you happy? Why do you eat apples?

6. HOW - For Way or Manner 🛠️

Use "How" to ask about the way something is done or the condition of something.

Examples: How do you play? How are you? How do you make a cake?

✏️ Using Question Words Practice

How to Use Question Words

Question words usually come at the BEGINNING of a question, before the helper verb.

Question Word Pattern 🎯

Question Word + Helper Verb + Subject + Main Verb + Object

Example: What | do | you | like | ?

Examples with Different Question Words 📚

What
What do you like?
Who
Who is your best friend?
Where
Where do you live?
When
When do you eat breakfast?
Why
Why do you play soccer?
How
How are you today?

Matching Question Words to Answers 🎯

The answer you want determines which question word to use:

Want to know WHAT? Use "What"
Want to know WHO? Use "Who"
Want to know WHERE? Use "Where"

WHAT Questions 📦

WHO Questions 👥

Question

WHERE Questions 📍

Question

Answer
I live in Jakarta.
Question

Answer

WHEN and WHY Questions ⏰🤔

When Question
When do you eat breakfast?
When Answer
I eat breakfast at 7 o'clock.
Why Question
Why do you like soccer?
Why Answer
Because it is fun!
