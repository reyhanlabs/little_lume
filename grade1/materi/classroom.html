"A" or "AN"? — The Article Rule
📌 Use "A"
before consonant sounds
📌 Use "AN"
before vowel sounds (a,e,i,o,u)
💡 Rule: Say "A" before words that start with B, C, D, F, G, H... and most consonants. Say "AN" before words that start with A, E, I, O, U!
