GRADE 1
Greetings Adventure 👋

Hi! I'm Lume! ✨

I'm a little fairy who shines all day long — from morning until night! Come and join me to learn how to say greetings in English. There are 7 fun games waiting for you. Collect stars at every level, and earn your certificate at the end of the adventure!

Level 1 · Matching

The Right Time! ⏰

Click one of the greeting sentences, then click the time picture that matches.

Level 2 · Multiple Choice

The Right Words 🎯

Read the situation, then choose the best greeting.

Level 3 · Fill in the Blank

Complete the Sentence! ✏️

Click a word from the word bank to fill in the blank.

Level 4 · True or False

True or False? 🤔

Press 👍 if the sentence is True, or 👎 if it is False.

Level 5 · Mini Game

Pop the Greeting Bubbles! 🫧

Click ONLY the bubbles that have a greeting word. Don't click the other words!

Level 6 · Put in Order

A Full Day in Order 🌅➜🌙

Click the greetings in time order, from morning to night.

Level 7 · Question & Answer

The Perfect Pair 💬

Match each phrase or question with the correct answer.

All Done!

🎉 Great Job! 🎉
