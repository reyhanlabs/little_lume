💬 Sample Dialogue

🧒 Budi
Good morning! My name is Budi. What is your name?
👧 Sari
Good morning, Budi! My name is Sari. Nice to meet you!
🧒 Budi
Nice to meet you too, Sari! How are you?
👧 Sari
I am fine, thank you! Goodbye!

🎮 Classroom Activities

1

👋 Hello Circle

Students stand in a circle. One by one they introduce themselves: "Hi! My name is ___." The others reply: "Nice to meet you, ___!"

2

🎭 Pair Role Play

Students work in pairs and act as new friends. Practise the full dialogue from greeting to farewell.

3

🎵 Greeting Song

Sing "Hello, Hello, How Are You?" together while waving to friends across the room.

💡 Teacher Tips

  • Use puppets or hand puppets to make the dialogue more engaging.
  • Praise every attempt: "Great job! Well done! ⭐"
  • Pair the vocabulary with physical actions — wave while saying "Hello!"

💬 Sample Sentences

🧒 Student
I have three pencils.
🍎 Teacher asks:
How many apples are there?
Student counts aloud: "One, two, three… five apples!"

🎮 Classroom Activities

1

🖐️ Count My Fingers!

The teacher holds up fingers; students call out the number in English together as a class.

2

🎲 Number Dice Game

Roll a die and say the number shown in English. A correct answer earns a star sticker!

3

🎵 Count with Me Song

Sing "One, Two, Three, Four, Five – Once I Caught a Fish Alive!" while counting on fingers.

💡 Teacher Tips

  • Use real objects (pencils, books, fruit) to make counting concrete.
  • Put a large number poster on the classroom wall for reference.
  • Make colourful number flashcards for daily drills.

🎮 Classroom Activities

1

🌈 Colour Hunt!

The teacher calls a colour in English; students race to touch an object of that colour in the classroom and shout its name.

2

🖍️ Colour & Say

Students colour a simple picture (fruit, balloons), then present it: "This is a balloon. It is yellow!"

3

🎵 Rainbow Song

Sing "I Can Sing a Rainbow" together while pointing to colour cards one by one.

💡 Teacher Tips

  • Prepare large, bright colour cards for every lesson.
  • Ask students to wear a specific colour on a chosen day.
  • Connect colours to real classroom objects: the board, books, chairs.

💬 Sample Dialogue

🧒 Student
I like rabbits. Rabbits are cute!
🐾 Teacher asks:
What animal is this?
Student answers: "It is a cat!"
🧒 Student A
What sound does a dog make?
👧 Student B
A dog says: Woof! Woof! 🐶

🎮 Classroom Activities

1

🎭 Animal Acting!

A student or teacher mimics an animal's sound and movement. Others guess in English: "Is it a cat?"

2

🃏 Flash Card Quiz

The teacher shows an animal picture card; students race to call out the animal's name in English.

3

🎨 Draw & Tell

Students draw their favourite animal and present it to the class: "This is my favourite animal. It is a ___."

💡 Teacher Tips

  • Use animal hand puppets or vivid picture cards.
  • Teach the animal sounds in English: meow, woof, moo, ribbit.
  • Link the topic to a simple illustrated animal storybook.
