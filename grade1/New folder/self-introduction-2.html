What is Self Introduction?

A self introduction is when you tell other people who you are! You share your name, your age, where you are from, and what you like. It's the first thing we say when we meet someone new. 😊

💡 Remember: Always smile when you introduce yourself! It makes people happy! 😄
🗣️
Key Phrases – What to Say!
👋
Hello! / Hi! / Good morning!
Greeting people when you first meet them
🏷️
My name is ___.
Tell people your name
🎂
I am ___ years old.
Tell people how old you are
🌍
I am from ___.
Tell people where you come from
❤️
I like ___.
Tell people what you enjoy!
🤝
Nice to meet you!
Say this at the end of your introduction
📝
Example Introduction
👋 Hello! My name is Sam.
🎂 I am seven years old.
🌍 I am from Indonesia.
🏫 I am in Grade 1.
❤️ I like drawing and playing football.
🤝 Nice to meet you!
💬
Mini Dialogue – Let's Talk!
👦
Sam: Hello! My name is Sam. What is your name?
👧
Lily: Hi, Sam! My name is Lily. Nice to meet you!
👦
Sam: Nice to meet you too, Lily! How old are you?
👧
Lily: I am six years old. How about you?
👦
Sam: I am seven! I like playing football. What do you like?
👧
Lily: I like drawing and singing! 🎨🎵
