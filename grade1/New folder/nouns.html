Noun = A Naming Word

A noun is a word that names someone or something. It can be a person, a place, an animal, or a thing. Look around you — everything you can point to and name is a noun!

teacher 🧑‍🏫 school 🏫 dog 🐶 ball ⚽
🧑‍🎓

Person

mom, friend, teacher, doctor
🏠

Place

school, park, home, zoo
🐶

Animal

dog, cat, bird, fish
🍔

Thing

ball, book, chair, apple
