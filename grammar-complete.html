📝 Noun (Kata Benda)

Grade 1 - Basic Level

📚

What is a Noun?

Definition
A noun is a word that names a person, place, thing, or animal. Nouns are fundamental building blocks of English grammar.
Types of Nouns
1. Common Nouns: General names (cat, house, book)
2. Proper Nouns: Specific names (John, London, Sunday) - Always capitalized
3. Concrete Nouns: Things you can touch (apple, chair, water)
4. Abstract Nouns: Ideas or feelings (love, happiness, freedom)
📌 Examples
"I have a cat named Whiskers."
Cat (common noun), Whiskers (proper noun)
"The book is on the table."
Book dan table adalah benda yang dapat disentuh (concrete nouns)
"Love makes people happy."
Love dan happiness adalah ide/perasaan (abstract nouns)
💡 Important Notes
  • Proper nouns selalu dimulai dengan huruf capital
  • Nouns dapat menjadi subject atau object dalam sebuah kalimat
  • Kebanyakan nouns dapat plural dengan menambah -s atau -es

⚡ Verb (Kata Kerja)

Grade 1 - Basic Level

⚡

What is a Verb?

Definition
A verb is a word that shows action or state. Verbs tell us what someone or something is doing, or what condition they are in.
Basic Verb Forms:
run, eat, sleep, play, read, write, jump, dance, sing, walk
📌 Examples
"She runs every morning."
Runs = aksi lari yang dilakukan secara rutin
"I eat breakfast at 7 AM."
Eat = aksi makan
"They play football."
Play = aksi bermain

✨ Adjective (Kata Sifat)

Grade 1 - Basic Level

✨

What is an Adjective?

Definition
An adjective is a word that describes or modifies a noun. It tells us more information about a person, place, or thing.
Common Adjectives:
big, small, red, blue, happy, sad, fast, slow, hot, cold, beautiful, ugly, good, bad
📌 Examples
"The big dog is playing."
Big = mendeskripsikan ukuran anjing
"She has a red notebook."
Red = mendeskripsikan warna notebook
"The happy children are laughing."
Happy = mendeskripsikan perasaan anak-anak

👤 Pronoun (Kata Ganti)

Grade 1 - Basic Level

👤

What is a Pronoun?

Definition
A pronoun is a word that replaces a noun. Pronouns help us avoid repetition when we talk about people or things.
Subject Pronouns:
I, you, he, she, it, we, they
📌 Examples
"John is a student. He likes English."
He = mengganti John (subject pronoun)
"My sister and I go to school together. We walk every day."
We = mengganti my sister and I

🔗 Is/Am

Grade 1 - Basic Level

🔗

To Be: Am / Is / Are

Definition
"To be" adalah verb yang paling penting dalam Bahasa Inggris. Digunakan untuk menunjukkan identitas, kondisi, atau keberadaan.
Formula:
I am / You are / He is / She is / It is / We are / They are
📌 Examples
"I am a student." (Saya adalah seorang siswa)
Subject: I → Verb: am
"She is happy." (Dia senang)
Subject: She → Verb: is
"They are teachers." (Mereka adalah guru)
Subject: They → Verb: are
💡 Important Notes
  • I → am
  • He, She, It → is
  • You, We, They → are

📅 Present Simple Tense

Grade 2

📅

Present Simple: Habitual Actions

Definition
Present Simple digunakan untuk menyatakan kebiasaan, fakta umum, rutinitas, atau hal-hal yang selalu benar.
Formula (Affirmative):
Subject + Verb (base form/+s) + Object

Examples:
I/You/We/They + play
He/She/It + plays
📌 Examples
"I play football every Saturday."
Kebiasaan: bermain sepak bola setiap hari Sabtu
"She drinks coffee in the morning."
Rutinitas: minum kopi di pagi hari
"The Earth goes around the Sun."
Fakta umum: Bumi mengelilingi Matahari
💡 Important Notes
  • Untuk he/she/it: tambahkan -s atau -es pada verb
  • Sering digunakan dengan adverbs: always, usually, often, sometimes, never
  • Untuk negative: do/does + not + verb

⏰ Past Simple Tense

Grade 3

⏰

Past Simple: Completed Actions

Definition
Past Simple digunakan untuk menyatakan tindakan yang telah selesai di masa lalu. Digunakan dengan waktu spesifik (yesterday, last week, etc).
Formula (Affirmative):
Subject + Verb (past form) + Object

Regular verbs: verb + -ed (played, walked, studied)
Irregular verbs: went, ate, saw, had, was, did
📌 Examples
"I played football yesterday."
Aksi bermain yang sudah selesai kemarin
"She went to Paris last summer."
Went = irregular verb dari go
"They watched a movie last night."
Watched = regular verb + -ed
💡 Important Notes
  • Regular verbs: tambahkan -ed (worked, studied, played)
  • Irregular verbs perlu dihafal (go-went, eat-ate, see-saw)
  • Time markers: yesterday, last week, 2 days ago, in 2020

🎬 Present Continuous Tense

Grade 3

🎬

Present Continuous: Ongoing Actions NOW

Definition
Present Continuous digunakan untuk tindakan yang sedang berlangsung SEKARANG, pada saat kita berbicara.
Formula:
Subject + am/is/are + Verb + -ing

Examples:
I am playing
She is reading
They are studying
📌 Examples
"I am eating lunch right now."
Sedang makan sekarang (aksi berlangsung)
"She is watching TV at the moment."
Sedang menonton TV saat ini
"We are learning English today."
Sedang belajar bahasa Inggris hari ini
💡 Important Notes
  • Gunakan be verb (am/is/are) + -ing form
  • Time markers: now, right now, at the moment, today
  • Sering digunakan dengan "Look!" atau "Listen!"

🔀 Conditional Sentences (If...)

Grade 5

🔀

If Clauses: Real & Unreal Conditions

Definition
Conditional sentences digunakan untuk membicarakan situasi yang kemungkinan terjadi atau tidak mungkin terjadi, dengan konsekuensi-nya.
Tiga Tipe Conditional
Type 1 (Real): Kemungkinan terjadi di masa depan
Type 2 (Unreal): Bayangan situasi di masa sekarang
Type 3 (Impossible): Situasi yang tidak mungkin terjadi di masa lalu
Type 1: If + Present Simple → Future Simple
If you study hard, you will pass the exam.
Type 2: If + Past Simple → would + verb
If you were rich, you would travel around the world.
Type 3: If + Past Perfect → would have + past participle
If you had studied, you would have passed the exam.
📌 Examples
"If it rains tomorrow, I will stay at home."
Type 1: Kemungkinan nyata di masa depan
"If I were a bird, I would fly to the sky."
Type 2: Situasi bayangan (tidak nyata) sekarang
"If I had known about the party, I would have come."
Type 3: Menyesal tentang masa lalu

💪 Modal Verbs

Grade 5

💪

Expressing Ability, Permission, Obligation

Definition
Modal verbs digunakan untuk mengekspresikan kemampuan, izin, kewajiban, atau kemungkinan. Modal verbs tidak berubah bentuk.
Jenis-Jenis Modal Verbs
can: Kemampuan (I can swim)
could: Kemampuan masa lalu (She could dance)
must: Kewajiban kuat (You must come)
should: Saran/nasihat (You should rest)
may: Izin formal (May I help?)
might: Kemungkinan (It might rain)
will: Masa depan (I will come)
would: Kemungkinan hypothetical (I would go if...)
Formula:
Subject + Modal Verb + Base Verb + Object
📌 Examples
"I can speak English fluently."
Can = kemampuan
"You must finish your homework."
Must = kewajiban/keharusan
"You should drink more water."
Should = saran/nasihat

🔄 Passive Voice

Grade 5

🔄

Shifting Focus from Subject to Object

Definition
Passive voice digunakan ketika kita ingin memusatkan perhatian pada aksi (objek) daripada orang yang melakukan aksi (subjek).
Formula:
Object + be verb + past participle + by + subject (optional)
📌 Examples
Active: "The teacher wrote the report."
Passive: "The report was written by the teacher."
Fokus bergeser dari teacher ke report
Active: "John cleaned the house."
Passive: "The house was cleaned by John."
Fokus ke objek (house) daripada subjek (John)
💡 Important Notes
  • Gunakan be verb yang sesuai dengan waktu: is/are (present), was/were (past)
  • "By" clause opsional jika sudah jelas siapa yang melakukan
  • Past participle berbeda untuk setiap tense

🔗 Relative Clause

Grade 5

🔗

Who, Which, That: Combining Sentences

Definition
Relative clause menghubungkan dua kalimat dengan menggunakan relative pronouns (who, which, that, whose, where) untuk memberikan informasi tambahan tentang noun.
Relative Pronouns
who: Untuk orang (subject atau object)
which: Untuk benda/binatang
that: Bisa untuk orang atau benda
whose: Menunjukkan kepemilikan
where: Untuk tempat
📌 Examples
"The girl who studies English is my sister."
Who = relative pronoun untuk orang (subject)
"The book which I read last week was interesting."
Which = relative pronoun untuk benda
"This is the place where I was born."
Where = relative pronoun untuk tempat

🎭 Subjunctive Mood

Grade 6 - Advanced

🎭

Expressing Wishes, Suggestions, and Requirements

Definition
Subjunctive mood digunakan untuk mengekspresikan keinginan, saran, atau persyaratan yang tidak (belum) menjadi kenyataan. Dalam bahasa Inggris modern, subjunctive mood jarang digunakan tapi masih penting dalam konteks formal.
Bentuk Subjunctive:
"I wish that I were a doctor."
"It is important that she be on time."
"I suggest that he study harder."
📌 Examples
"I wish I were taller." (Padahal saya tidak tinggi)
Were = subjunctive form (bukan was)
"It is crucial that she attend the meeting."
Attend = base form (bukan attends)

↔️ Inversion

Grade 6 - Advanced

↔️

Changing Word Order for Emphasis

Definition
Inversion adalah perubahan urutan kata (mengurangi subjek dan auxilary verb) untuk emphasis atau style tertentu. Umumnya digunakan dalam bahasa formal atau sastra.
Jenis Inversion
1. Dengan negative adverbs: Never have I seen such a thing.
2. Dengan "had": Had I known, I would have helped.
3. Dengan "should": Should you need help, call me.
4. Dengan "may/might": May you live long!
📌 Examples
"Never have I heard such beautiful music."
Normal: I have never heard... → Inversion untuk emphasis
"Had I been there, I would have helped you."
Normal: If I had been... → Inversion formal/literary

💬 Reported Speech (Indirect Speech)

Grade 6 - Advanced

💬

Quoting Someone Indirectly

Definition
Reported speech digunakan untuk melaporkan apa yang dikatakan orang lain tanpa menggunakan quotation marks. Beberapa perubahan tenses dan pronouns diperlukan.
Perubahan Tenses (Backshift)
Present Simple → Past Simple: "I eat" → He said he ate
Present Continuous → Past Continuous: "I am reading" → She said she was reading
Will → Would: "I will come" → He said he would come
Can → Could: "I can help" → She said she could help
📌 Examples
Direct: "I love English," she said.
Reported: She said that she loved English.
Tense berubah: present → past (loved)
Direct: "Will you come tomorrow?" he asked.
Reported: He asked if I would come the next day.
Will → would, tomorrow → the next day
💡 Important Notes
  • Time expressions juga berubah: today → that day, tomorrow → the next day
  • Pronouns bisa berubah tergantung konteks
  • Untuk pertanyaan, gunakan if/whether

📍 Word Order

Grade 6 - Advanced

📍

Standard & Advanced Word Order Patterns

Basic Word Order (SVO)
Bahasa Inggris mengikuti pattern: Subject - Verb - Object (Pelaku - Tindakan - Penerima Tindakan)
Standard English Word Order:
Subject + Auxiliary Verb + Main Verb + Object + Prepositional Phrase

Example:
She has been reading a book in the library.
Adverb Placement
Frequency adverbs: Sebelum main verb (usually, often, always, never)
"I always drink coffee in the morning."

Manner adverbs: Setelah object atau di akhir kalimat
"She speaks English fluently."
📌 Examples
"The cat sat on the mat." (SVO)
Subject: The cat, Verb: sat, Object: on the mat
"She has already finished her work."
Already = frequency adverb, ditempatkan sebelum main verb
"Yesterday, I went to the market with my mother."
Time expression di depan untuk emphasis

🎯 Imperative

Grade 2 - Basic

🎯

Commands & Instructions (Perintah & Instruksi)

Definisi
Imperative adalah bentuk kata kerja yang digunakan untuk memberikan perintah, instruksi, atau permintaan langsung kepada seseorang.
Formula:
Verb (Base Form) + Object/Complement

Contoh:
Close the door. / Tutup pintu.
Sit down. / Duduk.
📌 Examples
"Open the window!"
Buka jendela! (Perintah langsung)
"Please help me."
Tolong bantu saya. (Permintaan sopan)
"Don't run!"
Jangan lari! (Perintah negatif)

🔢 Plural Nouns

Grade 2 - Basic

🔢

Membentuk Kata Benda Jamak (Plural Forms)

Definisi
Plural noun adalah bentuk kata benda yang menunjukkan lebih dari satu benda atau makhluk hidup.
Aturan Dasar:
1. Kebanyakan: Noun + s → cats, dogs, books
2. Berakhir -s, -x, -z, -ch, -sh: + es → boxes, buses
3. Berakhir consonant + y: Ubah y → ies → baby → babies
4. Berakhir -f atau -fe: Ubah → ves → knife → knives
5. Irregular: child → children, man → men
📌 Examples
cat → cats, dog → dogs
Aturan dasar: tambah -s
box → boxes, glass → glasses
Berakhir -x, -s: tambah -es
baby → babies, lady → ladies
Consonant + y: ubah jadi -ies

🚀 Adverb (Basic)

Grade 2 - Basic

🚀

Kata Keterangan Dasar (Basic Adverbs)

Definisi
Adverb adalah kata yang menjelaskan verb, adjective, atau adverb lain. Kebanyakan dibentuk dengan menambahkan -ly pada adjective.
Cara Membentuk:
Adjective + ly = Adverb

quick → quickly
slow → slowly
happy → happily
📌 Examples
"She runs quickly."
Dia berlari dengan cepat. (quickly = manner adverb)
"He speaks softly."
Dia berbicara dengan lembut.
"I work hard."
Saya bekerja keras. (hard = adverb tidak berakhir -ly)

❓ Question Formation

Grade 3 - Intermediate

❓

Membentuk Pertanyaan (Question Patterns)

Definisi
Dalam bahasa Inggris, pertanyaan dibentuk dengan menginversi posisi auxiliary verb dan subject, atau menggunakan question words.
Pola Pertanyaan:
1. Yes/No Questions: Auxiliary + Subject + Verb?
"Do you like coffee?" "Are they coming?"

2. Wh- Questions: Question Word + Auxiliary + Subject + Verb?
"Where do you live?" "What are you doing?"
📌 Examples
"Do you play soccer?"
Kamu bermain sepak bola? (Yes/No question)
"Where are you going?"
Kemana kamu pergi? (Wh- question)
"When did they arrive?"
Kapan mereka tiba? (Wh- question dengan past)

👫 Object Pronouns

Grade 3 - Intermediate

👫

Kata Ganti Obyek (Object Pronouns)

Definisi
Object pronouns adalah kata ganti yang digunakan sebagai objek dari kata kerja atau preposisi. Contoh: me, you, him, her, it, us, them.
Object Pronouns Chart:
I → me
You → you
He → him
She → her
It → it
We → us
They → them
📌 Examples
"She gave me a book."
Dia memberi saya sebuah buku. (me = object pronoun)
"I love them."
Saya mencintai mereka. (them = object pronoun)
"Please help us."
Tolong bantu kami. (us = object pronoun)

✅ Present Perfect

Grade 4 - Intermediate

✅

Waktu Sekarang Sempurna (Present Perfect Tense)

Definisi
Present Perfect menunjukkan tindakan yang dimulai di masa lalu tapi masih memiliki pengaruh di sekarang atau baru saja selesai.
Formula:
Have/Has + Past Participle

Subject (I, You, We, They) + Have + V3
Subject (He, She, It) + Has + V3

Example:
"I have finished my homework." / "She has seen that movie."
📌 Examples
"I have been to Paris twice."
Saya telah pergi ke Paris dua kali. (Pengalaman)
"She has completed her project."
Dia telah menyelesaikan proyeknya. (Baru selesai)
"They have lived here for 5 years."
Mereka telah tinggal di sini selama 5 tahun. (Berlanjut)

🌀 Past Continuous

Grade 4 - Intermediate

🌀

Waktu Lampau Sedang Berlangsung (Past Continuous Tense)

Definisi
Past Continuous menunjukkan tindakan yang sedang berlangsung pada waktu tertentu di masa lalu.
Formula:
Was/Were + Verb-ing

I/He/She/It + Was + V-ing
You/We/They + Were + V-ing

Example:
"I was reading when she called." / "They were playing football yesterday."
📌 Examples
"I was sleeping when the phone rang."
Saya sedang tidur ketika telepon berdering.
"She was cooking dinner at 6 PM."
Dia sedang memasak makan malam pada jam 6 sore.
"We were watching a movie while they were studying."
Kami sedang menonton film sementara mereka sedang belajar.

⚖️ Comparatives

Grade 4 - Intermediate

⚖️

Membandingkan Dua Hal (Comparative Adjectives)

Definisi
Comparative adjectives digunakan untuk membandingkan dua orang, benda, atau hal yang berbeda.
Cara Membentuk:
1. Short adjectives (-er form): tall → taller, fast → faster
2. Long adjectives (more + adjective): beautiful → more beautiful
3. Irregular: good → better, bad → worse

Pattern: Subject + Verb + Comparative + than + Object
📌 Examples
"John is taller than Mary."
John lebih tinggi daripada Mary.
"This car is more expensive than that one."
Mobil ini lebih mahal daripada yang itu.
"English is easier than Chinese for me."
Bahasa Inggris lebih mudah daripada Bahasa Mandarin bagi saya.

🏆 Superlatives

Grade 4 - Intermediate

🏆

Tingkatan Tertinggi (Superlative Adjectives)

Definisi
Superlative adjectives digunakan untuk menunjukkan bahwa sesuatu memiliki kualitas tertinggi atau terendah di antara yang lain.
Cara Membentuk:
1. Short adjectives (-est form): tall → the tallest, fast → the fastest
2. Long adjectives (the most + adjective): beautiful → the most beautiful
3. Irregular: good → the best, bad → the worst

Pattern: Subject + Verb + The + Superlative + Noun
📌 Examples
"She is the smartest student in the class."
Dia adalah murid paling pintar di kelas.
"Mount Everest is the highest mountain in the world."
Gunung Everest adalah gunung tertinggi di dunia.
"This is the most beautiful sunset I've ever seen."
Ini adalah matahari terbenam paling indah yang pernah saya lihat.
