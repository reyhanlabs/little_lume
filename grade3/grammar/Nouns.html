What is a Noun?
📦

A noun is a word that names a person, place, thing, or animal!

👩 teacher 🏫 school 📚 book 🐶 dog 💡 idea ❤️ love
💡 Remember: If you can see it, touch it, feel it, or think about it — it's probably a noun! Everything around us has a name.

Common vs Proper Nouns

📌 Common Nouns

General names for things

  • 📖 book
  • 🏙️ city
  • 👨 teacher
  • 🐱 cat

📌 Proper Nouns

Specific names (always capitalize!)

  • 📖 Harry Potter
  • 🏙️ Jakarta
  • 👨 Mr. Rudi
  • 🐱 Milo
💡 Rule: Common nouns are general (dog, city). Proper nouns are specific and always start with a capital letter (Rover, London, Mrs. Smith)!
🔢
Singular vs Plural Nouns

One = Singular | More than one = Plural

💡 Rule: Most nouns add -s or -es to become plural. But some are irregular (child → children, mouse → mice, foot → feet)!
🧮
Countable vs Uncountable Nouns

✅ Countable

Things you can count!

🍎 1 apple, 2 apples
✏️ 1 pencil, 3 pencils

❌ Uncountable

Things you cannot count!

💧 water, 🥛 milk, 🧂 salt
💡 information, ❤️ love

💡 Tip: Uncountable nouns don't have a plural form. We say "some water" not "two waters"!
