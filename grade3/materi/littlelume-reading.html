STEP 3

Read the Story 📖

Mia and the Lost Kitten
👧🐱🌳🏡

On Saturday morning, Mia was playing in her garden. Suddenly, she heard a tiny sound: "Meow! Meow!" She looked under the big tree and found a small, gray kitten. It looked hungry and scared.

Mia gently picked up the kitten and brought it home. She gave it warm milk and a soft blanket. The kitten was happy and began to purr. Mia named the kitten "Cloud" because its fur was soft and gray like a cloud.

The next day, Mia and her mom made posters to find Cloud's real owner. They walked around the neighborhood and put up the posters. That evening, a little boy named Sam saw the poster and called Mia's house. Cloud was his kitten! He was very happy to see Cloud again.

Sam thanked Mia for taking care of his kitten. Mia felt proud and happy because she had helped a friend in need.
