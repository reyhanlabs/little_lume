🎯 What is Past Simple? Foundation

When we say "I played football yesterday" or "She went to the park," we use the Past Simple tense. It tells us about things that happened in the past!

What is Past Simple Tense?

The Past Simple tense describes actions that finished in the past. It's used for things that already happened!

💡 Past Simple Structure Grammar

Regular & Irregular Verbs

Most verbs add -ed, but some are special!

Verb + ed
or
Irregular
= Past Simple

Regular Verbs: Add -ed

play → played, want → wanted, walk → walked

Irregular Verbs: Special Forms

go → went, eat → ate, see → saw, have → had

✏️ When to Use Past Simple Practice

Finished Actions in the Past

Last week:
