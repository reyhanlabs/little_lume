Imperatives Commands

When your teacher says "Sit down!" or "Open your book!", they're using imperatives. Imperatives are commands and instructions!

What are Imperatives?

Imperatives are sentences that give commands or instructions. They tell someone to DO something!

How to Use Imperatives Grammar

Basic Imperative Form

Start with the base verb: Sit! Open! Read! Write!

Polite Imperatives

Add "Please": Please sit down. Please open your book.

Examples Sentences

Commands at School

Command:
Open your books!
Polite:
Please listen to me.
Instructions:
Write your name on the paper.
