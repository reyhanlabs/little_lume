Possessives Ownership

When we say "This is my book" or "Her name is Ani," we show who owns or has something. These are called possessives!

What are Possessives?

Possessives show ownership. They tell us WHO something belongs to!

Possessive Adjectives Grammar

My, Your, His, Her, Its, Our, Their

My book, Your pen, His bag, Her doll, Its tail, Our class, Their toys

Possessive 's

Add 's to show ownership: Ali's book, Cat's tail, Maria's friend

Examples Sentences

Showing Ownership

Example:
This is my pencil.
Example:

Example:
