Questions Asking

When we want to know something, we ask questions! "What's your name?" or "Do you like pizza?" are questions!

What are Questions?

Questions are sentences that ask for information. They always end with a question mark (?)

Question Types Grammar

Yes/No Questions

Do you like pizza? Is she happy? Can you swim?

Wh- Questions

What? Who? When? Where? Why? How?

Examples Sentences
