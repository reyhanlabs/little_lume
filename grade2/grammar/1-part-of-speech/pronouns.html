🎯 What Are Pronouns? Foundation

Do you know that instead of saying "Budi is playing with a ball," we can say "He is playing with a ball"? Words like he, she, it, I, you, we, and they are called pronouns!

Why Do Pronouns Matter?

Pronouns are special words that replace nouns so we don't have to repeat the same names over and over again. They make our sentences shorter and more interesting to read.

A pronoun is a word that takes the place of a noun. Instead of saying "Ani" every time, we can say "She."

💡 The Seven Pronouns Structure

The 7 Personal Pronouns

In English, there are 7 main personal pronouns we use:

Pronoun Meaning Example
I Myself (always capital) I am happy.
You The person I'm talking to You are kind.
He A boy or man He plays football.
She A girl or woman She reads books.
It A thing or animal It is big.
We Me and other people We like school.
They More than one person or thing They are friends.

Different pronouns for different amounts
Singular = 1 person | Plural = 2+ people/things

Singular (1)

  • I - myself
  • You - one person
  • He - one boy/man
  • She - one girl/woman
  • It - one thing

Plural (Many)

  • We - me + others
  • You - many people
  • They - 2+ people/things
  • They - 2+ girls/women
  • They - 2+ things

✏️ How to Use Pronouns Practice

When to Use Each Pronoun

Each pronoun has a specific use. Learning when to use each one is important!

I - About Yourself

Use "I" when you are talking about yourself. Remember: "I" is always written with a capital letter!

Example:
I am a student.
Example:
I like to play.

You - About Someone You're Talking To

Use "you" when you are talking directly to someone.

Example:
You are my friend.
Example:
You are very smart.

He - About a Boy or Man

Use "he" when talking about a boy or man.

Example:
He is my brother.
Example:
He plays football every day.

She - About a Girl or Woman

Use "she" when talking about a girl or woman.

Example:
She is my sister.
Example:
She likes to draw pictures.

It - About Things and Animals

Use "it" when talking about things, animals, or objects.

Example:
It is a beautiful dog.
Example:
It is raining today.

We - About a Group Including Me

Use "we" when you are part of a group.

Example:
We are classmates.
Example:
We like to play together.

They - About Multiple People or Things

Use "they" when talking about more than one person or thing.

Example:
They are my friends.
Example:
They like to play football.

📚 Real Examples Sentences

Pronouns in Sentences

Sentence:
I have a pencil. It is red.
Sentence:
She is my friend. She is very kind.
Sentence:
He likes to read. He reads many books.
Sentence:
We are in Grade 2. We study together.
Sentence:
They are my cousins. They visit us every month.

Replacing Nouns with Pronouns

With Noun

  • Budi is smart
  • Ani plays football
  • The cat sleeps
  • My parents are home

With Pronoun

  • He is smart
  • She plays football
  • It sleeps
  • They are home

💬 Conversation Speaking

Listen to how Doni and his friends use pronouns:

Doni:
"Hi! I am Doni. Who are you?"
Rini:
"I am Rini. This is my brother Adi. He is seven years old."
Doni:
"Nice to meet you, Rini and Adi! That is a cute dog. What is its name?"
Adi:
"It is called Buddy. He is very friendly. We love him very much!"
Doni:
"That's wonderful! Maybe we can play together with Buddy. What do you think?"
Rini:
"Great idea! We can play in the park. They have a nice area for dogs there. Are you free today?"
Doni:
"Yes, I am free! Let's go now!"
