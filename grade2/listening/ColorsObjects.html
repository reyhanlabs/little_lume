📌 Lesson 1 – Basic Colors
🌈 The Rainbow Colors
Colors are everywhere! In Grade 2, you learn the 10 most common color names in English. Listen carefully to how each color sounds when your teacher says it. Repeat each color after you hear it!

ORANGE 🟠

🎨 Colors in Nature & Life
  • RED - apple 🍎, fire 🔥, heart ❤️
  • YELLOW - sun ☀️, banana 🍌, flower 🌻
  • BLUE - sky 🌤️, ocean 🌊, water 💧
  • GREEN - grass 🌱, leaf 🍃, tree 🌳
  • ORANGE - orange 🍊, carrot 🥕
👂 Color Word Examples

"This is a red apple." (apple = merah)
"The sky is blue." (sky = langit)
"I like the green color." (warna hijau)
"My favorite color is purple." (favorit = kesukaan)

👂 Practice – Listen & Point
  1. Your teacher says a color (red, blue, green...). You point to something that color in your classroom.
  2. Listen to: "Show me something red / blue / yellow / green." Point and say "This is red!"
  3. Repeat each color after your teacher: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple, pink, brown, black, white.
  4. What colors do you see? Say it: "I see red. I see blue. I see green."
📌 Lesson 2 – Asking About Colors
🎨 "What Color Is...?"
The question "What color is...?" asks you to identify the color of an object. When you hear this question, listen to the object name, look at its color, and answer with the color word!
👂 Listen to Color Questions & Answers

Question: "What color is the apple?"
Answer: "It is red." (It = itu)

Question: "What color is the sky?"
Answer: "It is blue."

Question: "What color is your book?"
Answer: "My book is green."

📚 Practice Questions
  • "What color is the sun?" → "It is yellow."
  • "What color is the grass?" → "It is green."
  • "What color is your pencil?" → "My pencil is blue."
  • "What color is your bag?" → "My bag is black."
  • "What color is the teacher's shirt?" → "It is white."
💬 Sample Conversation

Teacher: "Children, look at this apple. What color is it?"
Student 1: "It is red!"
Teacher: "Very good! And what color is this banana?"
Student 2: "The banana is yellow!"
Teacher: "Excellent! Very smart!"

👂 Practice – Answer Color Questions
  1. Teacher asks: "What color is the board?" You answer: "It is (black/white/green)."
  2. Listen to 5 color questions and answer each one.
  3. Point to an object and your friend asks: "What color is it?" You answer with the color.
  4. Ask your classmate: "What color is your shirt?" and listen to their answer.
📌 Lesson 3 – Classroom Objects
📚 Things in Our Classroom
You see many objects every day in your classroom: desk, chair, book, pen, pencil, bag, window, door, etc. Listen to the object names carefully. These words are very important for daily school life.
🎧 Listen to Classroom Objects
  • BOOK 📖 - You read from this
  • PEN ✒️ - You write with this (has ink)
  • PENCIL ✏️ - You write with this (has lead)
  • DESK 📚 - You sit and work at this
  • CHAIR 🪑 - You sit on this
  • BAG 🎒 - You carry your books in this
  • ERASER 🧹 - You erase pencil marks with this
  • RULER 📏 - You draw straight lines with this
  • DOOR 🚪 - You enter through this
  • WINDOW 🪟 - You see outside through this
🎨 Objects Dialogue

Teacher: "Point to the desk."
Student: (points to desk)

Teacher: "Show me your pencil."
Student: (shows pencil)

📚 Object Sentences

"I have a book." (Saya punya buku)
"This is my desk." (Ini meja saya)
"Where is my pen?" (Mana penku?)
"I see a window." (Saya lihat jendela)

👂 Practice – Recognize & Point
  1. Teacher says "Show me a pen." You point to or hold up a pen.
  2. Listen: "Where is the door/window/desk/chair?" Point to it.
  3. Teacher says object names. You say what each object is for: "A pen is for writing."
  4. Look around your classroom and name objects you see in English.
📌 Lesson 4 – Colors + Objects Together
🎨 Describing Things
When you describe something, you say both the color AND the object name together. For example: "red apple" (not just "red" or just "apple"). Listen for how we put color words and object names together!
👂 Listen to Color + Object

Color first, then object:
"a red apple" 🍎
"a blue pen" ✒️
"a green book" 📖
"a brown desk" 📚
"a yellow pencil" ✏️
"a white board" 📋
"a black chair" 🪑

💬 Conversation with Descriptions

Friend 1: "I have a red book."
Friend 2: "Wow! A red book is nice!"

Teacher: "Can you find a blue pen?"
Student: "Yes! I have a blue pen!"

Child: "Look! I have a yellow pencil."
Mother: "That's a pretty yellow pencil!"

🎨 My School Things

"I have a red pencil." (My pencil is red)
"This is my blue bag." (My bag is blue)
"I like this green book." (This book is green)
"Look at my yellow pen!" (My pen is yellow)

👂 Practice – Color + Object
  1. Listen: "I have a blue pencil." What color? What object? Answer: blue pencil.
  2. Your teacher shows an object. Say the color + object: "It is a (red) (pen)."
  3. Describe your own things: "I have a ___ (color) ___ (object)." Example: "I have a blue pen."
  4. Look at your classroom. Describe 5 things using color + object words.

💡 Tips for Learning Colors & Objects
🌈
Use Real Objects Point to real things and say colors. "This is a red book." Practice with real items!
🔍
Look Around Find 5 objects of each color. Red things, blue things, green things in your room.
👂
Listen & Repeat Hear the teacher say a color. Repeat it. Say it many times to remember!
🎨
Draw & Name Draw something and color it. Say the color + object: "I draw a blue flower."
🗣️
Ask Friends Ask classmates: "What color is your bag?" Listen and learn!
🌟
Make Sentences Put color + object together: "I have a green pen." / "This is a yellow pencil."
