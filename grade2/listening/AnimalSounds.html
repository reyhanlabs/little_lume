Animals are wonderful! Each animal has its own name and sound. In this module, you will practice listening to animal names and the sounds they make. This is fun and helps you remember better! Let's listen to the animals! 🐾

📌 Lesson 1 – Domestic Animals
🐕 Animals at Home
Domestic animals are animals we keep at home or on farms. Listen to the animal name and its sound. For example, a dog says "woof woof!" and a cat says "meow meow!". Different animals make different sounds!
🎧 Listen to Domestic Animals & Sounds
🐶
DOG
"Woof! Woof!"
🐱
CAT
"Meow! Meow!"
🐮
COW
"Moo! Moo!"
🐷
PIG
"Oink! Oink!"
🐑
SHEEP
"Baa! Baa!"
🐓
CHICKEN
"Cluck! Cluck!"
👂 Sentences About Domestic Animals
  • "I have a dog. The dog says woof woof!" (anjing berkata woof)
  • "A cat says meow. I like cats!" (kucing berkata meow)
  • "The cow gives milk. It says moo!" (sapi memberikan susu)
  • "On the farm, we see pigs, sheep, and chickens." (di farm kami lihat babi, domba, ayam)
💬 Conversation About Domestic Animals

Child: "Listen! What animal is that?"
Mother: "That's a dog! It says woof woof!"

Friend: "Do you have a cat?"
You: "Yes! My cat says meow meow! It is very cute!"

👂 Practice – Domestic Animals
  1. Teacher makes an animal sound (meow, woof, moo). You say the animal name.
  2. Listen to: "The ___ says moo." You say: "The cow says moo!"
  3. Repeat animal sounds after your teacher: Woof! Meow! Moo! Oink! Baa!
  4. Make animal sounds. Your classmate guesses what animal it is.
📌 Lesson 2 – Wild Animals
🦁 Animals in the Jungle
Wild animals live in the jungle, forest, or savanna. Some wild animals are big and strong, like lions and elephants. Listen to wild animal names and their sounds. They make different sounds than farm animals!
🎧 Listen to Wild Animals & Their Sounds
🦁
LION
"Roar! Roar!"
🐯
TIGER
"Roar! Growl!"
🐘
ELEPHANT
"Trumpet! Trumpet!"
🐒
MONKEY
"Ooh! Ah! Ah!"
🦒
GIRAFFE
(quiet, no sound)
🐻
BEAR
"Growl! Roar!"
👂 Sentences About Wild Animals
  • "The lion is big and strong. It roars very loud!" (singa mengaum keras)
  • "An elephant is the biggest animal. It says trumpet!" (gajah adalah hewan terbesar)
  • "Monkeys live in trees. They say ooh ooh ah ah!" (monyet hidup di pohon)
  • "Tigers are fast and dangerous. They roar like lions!" (harimau cepat dan berbahaya)
💬 Jungle Sound Dialogue

Teacher: "Listen! What animal is this?" (makes roaring sound)
Student: "Is it a lion?"
Teacher: "Yes! A lion roars!"

Child: "Elephants are so big!"
Friend: "Yes! And they trumpet very loud!"

👂 Practice – Wild Animals
  1. Your teacher makes a wild animal sound. You name the animal.
  2. Listen: "The lion says ____." You complete: "roar!"
  3. Which sound is this: meow, roar, trumpet, ooh ooh? Name the animal!
  4. Make wild animal sounds. Your classmates guess which animal.
📌 Lesson 3 – Birds & Water Animals
🐦 Animals in the Sky & Water
Birds fly in the sky and have beautiful sounds. Fish and water animals live in water. Listen to bird and water animal names and their sounds. Some are soft sounds, some are loud!
🎧 Listen to Birds & Water Animals
🦆
DUCK
"Quack! Quack!"
🦆
CHICKEN
"Cluck! Cluck!"
🦜
PARROT
"Squawk! Squawk!"
🕊️
DOVE
"Coo! Coo!"
🐠
FISH
(no sound, silent)
🐢
TURTLE
(mostly quiet)
👂 Sentences About Birds & Water Animals
  • "Ducks swim in the water. They say quack quack!" (bebek berenang di air)
  • "Birds sing beautiful songs in the morning." (burung menyanyi indah)
  • "Fish are silent. They don't make sounds." (ikan diam/tidak bersuara)
  • "Parrots are smart birds. They can copy sounds!" (burung beo pandai dan bisa meniru suara)
💬 Conversation About Birds

Child: "Listen to that sound! What is it?"
Father: "That's a duck! It says quack quack!"

Friend: "I love birds!"
You: "Yes! Parrots can talk. They are so smart!"

👂 Practice – Birds & Water Animals
  1. Your teacher makes a bird sound (quack, cluck, squawk). You name the bird!
  2. Listen: "This animal swims and says quack." What is it? (duck)
  3. Can you imitate bird sounds? Say: quack, cluck, coo, squawk!
  4. Draw a bird or fish and describe it in English with sounds (if any).
📌 Lesson 4 – Animal Sounds & Songs
🎵 Fun with Animal Sounds
Kids all over the world love songs about animals! Listen to animal rhymes and songs. These songs help you remember animal names and sounds. Singing is a fun way to learn English!
🎵 Popular Animal Song (Example)

"Old MacDonald Had a Farm"

Old MacDonald had a farm, E-I-E-I-O,
And on that farm he had a cow, E-I-E-I-O,
With a moo moo here and a moo moo there,
Here a moo, there a moo, everywhere a moo moo,
Old MacDonald had a farm, E-I-E-I-O!

(You can replace "cow/moo" with: pig/oink, duck/quack, dog/woof, etc.)

👂 Animal Name + Sound Patterns
  • A dog says woof! (Seekor anjing berkata woof!)
  • A cat says meow!
  • A cow says moo!
  • A pig says oink!
  • A duck says quack!
  • A lion says roar!
🎵 Simple Animal Rhyme

"What Does the Animal Say?"

What does the dog say? Woof, woof, woof!
What does the cat say? Meow, meow, meow!
What does the cow say? Moo, moo, moo!
All these sounds, I hear today!
What do the animals say? Say, say, say!

👂 Practice – Animal Songs & Sounds
  1. Sing "Old MacDonald Had a Farm" with different animals and sounds!
  2. Learn the animal name + sound pattern: "A dog says woof!"
  3. Your teacher says an animal name. You say and do the sound action.
  4. Make up your own short animal song or rhyme!

💡 Tips for Learning Animal Sounds
🎵
Sing Songs Sing "Old MacDonald" and other animal songs many times. Music helps memory!
🔊
Make the Sounds Imitate animal sounds loud and proud! Woof! Meow! Moo! Have fun!
🎬
Act It Out Do actions while saying sounds. Move like the animal while saying its sound!
📚
Read Animal Books Look at animal pictures and books. Name each animal in English.
🐾
Visit the Zoo If you can go to a zoo, listen to real animal sounds and say their English names!
🎥
Watch Animal Videos Watch videos of animals. Listen to real sounds and repeat animal names!
✅ Self-Check – Can You Do This?
  • I can listen to and identify 6 domestic animals and their sounds.
  • I can listen to and identify 6 wild animals and their sounds.
  • I can listen to and identify birds and water animals.
  • I can make animal sounds and say the animal name.
  • I understand sentences about what animals say.
  • I can sing "Old MacDonald Had a Farm" with different animals.
  • I can match animal sounds to their names in English.
