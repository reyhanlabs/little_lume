📌 Lesson 1 – Morning Activities
🌅 Morning Routine
Every morning, you do many things. Listen to the activity words that describe your morning. Common morning activities are: wake up, brush teeth, take a bath, get dressed, eat breakfast, and go to school.
🎧 Listen to Morning Activities
  • WAKE UP 🌅 - Open your eyes and get out of bed (bangun)
  • BRUSH TEETH 🪥 - Clean your teeth with a toothbrush (sikat gigi)
  • TAKE A BATH / SHOWER 🚿 - Wash your body with water (mandi)
  • GET DRESSED 👕 - Put on your clothes (berpakaian)
  • EAT BREAKFAST 🥞 - Eat food in the morning (sarapan)
  • GO TO SCHOOL 🏫 - Travel to your school (pergi ke sekolah)
🌅 Morning Timeline
6:00 AM
Wake up - Get out of bed
6:15 AM
Brush teeth - Brush your teeth
6:30 AM
Take a bath - Shower and get clean
6:50 AM
Get dressed - Put on your school uniform
7:00 AM
Eat breakfast - Eat rice/bread with egg
7:30 AM
Go to school - Travel to school
💬 Morning Conversation

Mother: "Good morning! Wake up!"
Child: "Good morning, Mom!"

Mother: "Have you brushed your teeth?"
Child: "Yes! I brushed my teeth."

Mother: "Hurry! Go take a bath!"
Child: "Okay, Mom! I'm taking a bath now!"

👂 Practice – Morning Actions
  1. Teacher says "Wake up!" You act it out (pretend to wake up).
  2. Listen to: "Brush your teeth / Get dressed / Eat breakfast." You do the action.
  3. Your teacher mixes up the order. You tell the correct morning order.
  4. Tell your partner your morning routine: "I wake up at 6 o'clock. I brush my teeth..."
📌 Lesson 2 – School Activities
🏫 At School
At school, you do many things with your teacher and friends. Listen to school activity words: study, read, write, listen, speak, play, sit, stand, answer questions, and raise your hand.
🎧 Listen to School Activities
  • STUDY 📚 - Learn new things from your teacher (belajar)
  • READ 📖 - Look at words and understand them (membaca)
  • WRITE ✏️ - Put words on paper with pen/pencil (menulis)
  • LISTEN 👂 - Hear what the teacher says (mendengarkan)
  • SPEAK 🗣️ - Say words out loud (berbicara)
  • PLAY 🎮 - Have fun with friends (bermain)
  • SIT DOWN 🪑 - Be in a seat (duduk)
  • STAND UP 🧍 - Get up from your seat (berdiri)
🏫 A School Day Timeline
7:30
Arrive at school - Come to school
8:00
Study - Learn English/Math
9:00
Read and write - Practice reading/writing
10:00
Break / Play - Rest and play with friends
10:30
Study more - Learn more subjects
12:00
Eat lunch - Eat at school
💬 School Dialogue

Teacher: "Children, sit down and listen!"
Students: (sit and listen)

Teacher: "Now, open your books and read page 5."
Student: "Can you help me write?"
Teacher: "Of course! Here, write this word."

👂 Practice – School Actions
  1. Teacher says "Sit down / Stand up / Listen / Speak." You do the action.
  2. Listen to school activity descriptions. Guess what activity it is.
  3. Tell your class what you do at school: "I study English. I read books. I play with friends."
  4. Play "School Day": Teacher gives commands. You follow each one in order.
📌 Lesson 3 – Afternoon & Evening Activities
🌆 After School & Evening
After school, you go home and do many activities. Listen to afternoon and evening activity words: go home, do homework, play outside, watch TV, eat dinner, take a bath, sleep.
🎧 Listen to Afternoon & Evening Activities
  • GO HOME 🏠 - Return to your house (pulang ke rumah)
  • DO HOMEWORK 📝 - Complete schoolwork at home (mengerjakan PR)
  • PLAY OUTSIDE 🏃 - Have fun in the yard/park (bermain di luar)
  • WATCH TV 📺 - See programs on television (menonton TV)
  • EAT DINNER 🍽️ - Eat food at evening (makan malam)
  • TAKE A BATH 🛁 - Wash before sleep (mandi malam)
  • GO TO BED 🛏️ - Lie down to sleep (pergi tidur)
  • SLEEP 😴 - Rest at night (tidur)
🌆 Afternoon & Evening Timeline
1:00 PM
Go home - Leave school and go to house
2:00 PM
Eat lunch - Have lunch at home
2:30 PM
Do homework - Complete school tasks
3:30 PM
Play outside - Have fun with friends
5:00 PM
Watch TV / Read - Relax (if allowed)
6:00 PM
Eat dinner - Family dinner time
7:00 PM
Take a bath - Wash before sleep
8:00 PM
Go to bed - Sleep for the night
👂 Practice – Evening Actions
  1. Tell your routine after school: "I go home. I do homework. I play..."
  2. Teacher says afternoon activities. You show the actions or draw them.
  3. Listen to a story about someone's afternoon. Answer: "What did they do?"
  4. Create your own evening routine and describe it in English.
📌 Lesson 4 – My Daily Routine
📅 A Full Day in Order
When we talk about a full day, we say activities in order - what we do first, second, third, etc. Use words like "First, Then, After that, Finally" to show the order of activities. This helps others understand your entire day!
📋 A Student's Daily Routine

"My Daily Routine"

First, I wake up at 6:00 AM. (wake up)
Then, I brush my teeth and take a bath. (brush teeth, take bath)
After that, I get dressed and eat breakfast. (get dressed, eat)
Next, I go to school by bus. (go to school)
At school, I study and play with friends. (study, play)
After school, I go home and do homework. (go home, homework)
Then, I play outside and watch TV. (play, watch TV)
Finally, I eat dinner, take a bath, and go to bed. (dinner, bath, sleep)
I sleep at 8:00 PM! (sleep)

💬 Conversation About Daily Routine

Mother: "Tell me about your school day!"
Child: "First, I listened to the teacher. Then, I read a book. After that, I played with my friends. Finally, I came home!"

Friend: "What do you do in the morning?"
You: "I wake up, brush my teeth, take a bath, get dressed, eat breakfast, and go to school!"

🎯 Time Words for Routine
  • First - The first activity (pertama)
  • Then / Next - The second activity (kemudian)
  • After that - The third activity (setelah itu)
  • Finally / At the end - The last activity (akhirnya)
👂 Practice – My Routine
  1. Listen to a person's daily routine. Write what they do and in what order.
  2. Tell your partner your daily routine using First, Then, After that, Finally.
  3. Your teacher mixes up activities. You put them in the correct order.
  4. Draw pictures of your day and describe each picture in English.

💡 Tips for Learning Daily Activities
⏰
Use Your Routine Your real routine is the best teacher! Say activity words as you do them.
🎭
Act Them Out Do the actions while saying the words. This helps you remember!
📝
Draw a Timeline Draw your daily schedule and label each activity in English.
🗣️
Tell Your Routine Practice telling your day to parents, friends, or teachers in English!
📚
Use Order Words Always use First, Then, After that, Finally when explaining your day.
🎧
Listen Every Day Ask your teacher to describe their daily routine. Listen carefully!
