What is the Alphabet?
📚

The alphabet has 26 letters!

We use letters to make words and sentences!

🔤 A is for Apple 🍎 🔤 B is for Ball ⚽ 🔤 C is for Cat 🐱
💡 Did you know? There are 26 letters in the English alphabet! 5 are vowels (A, E, I, O, U) and 21 are consonants!

Vowels & Consonants

🔴 VOWELS

The special letters that make open sounds!

A · E · I · O · U

(and sometimes Y)

🔵 CONSONANTS

All the other letters!

B · C · D · F · G · H · J · K · L · M · N · P · Q · R · S · T · V · W · X · Y · Z
💡 Remember: Vowels are the sound-makers! Every word needs at least one vowel to make a sound. Try saying "A-E-I-O-U" — it's like singing! 🎵
