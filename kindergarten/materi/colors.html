🌈 What Are Colors?

Colors are everywhere! 👀 We can see colors in the sky, in flowers, in animals, and in everyday things. Let's learn the names of colors in English! 🎨

📖 How to use colors in sentences

🔤 Sentence Pattern 1
The [object] is [color].
🌸 The flower is pink.
🍎 The apple is red.
🍋 The lemon is yellow.
🌿 The leaf is green.
🔤 Sentence Pattern 2
I see a [color] [object]!
🦋 I see a blue butterfly!
🐦 I see an orange bird!
🐱 I see a black cat!
🐇 I see a white rabbit!
🔤 Sentence Pattern 3
My favorite color is [color]!
💜 My favorite color is purple!
❤️ My favorite color is red!
💙 My favorite color is blue!
