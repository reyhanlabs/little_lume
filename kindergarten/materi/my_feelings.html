📚 GRAMMAR FOCUS 1

MY + Name + IS

MY means it belongs to me! We use MY + NAME + IS to tell someone our name.

My name is ___

Examples:

🧒 My name is Ben.   👧 My name is Mia.

📚 GRAMMAR FOCUS 2

I + AM + Feeling Word

We use I AM + an adjective (a feeling word) to say how we feel!

I am ___

Example: I am happy! 😄

📖 Feelings Dictionary — let's learn what each feeling means!

😊Happy

You feel good and want to smile.

"I got a new toy — I am happy!"

😢Sad

You feel like crying. Something is not nice.

"My toy broke. I am sad."

🤩Excited

You feel super happy about something fun coming!

"It's my birthday! I am excited!"

😠Angry

You feel mad, like something is unfair.

"He took my crayon. I am angry."

😴Tired

Your body feels sleepy and wants to rest.

"It's late. I am tired."

😨Scared

You feel afraid, like in a dark room.

"I hear a loud noise. I am scared."

😲Surprised

Something happens that you did not expect!

"A party for me? I am surprised!"

😳Shy

You feel a little nervous to talk to new people.

"A new friend! I am shy."

😎Proud

You feel great about something you did well.

"I tied my shoes! I am proud!"

😟Worried

You feel a little scared about what might happen.

"I have a test. I am worried."

🥱Bored

You have nothing fun to do right now.

"No games today. I am bored."

🤪Silly

You feel funny and want to laugh and play!

"Let's make funny faces! I am silly."
