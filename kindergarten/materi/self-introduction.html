🗣️ Unit 4

Let's Practice a Conversation!

🧑‍🤝‍🧑

Meeting a New Friend

Practice with a partner — take turns!

🧒
Hi! My name is Sam. What's your name?
👧
Hello, Sam! My name is Mia.
🧒
How old are you, Mia?
👧
I am 5 years old. How old are you?
🧒
I am 5 years old too! Nice to meet you!
👧
Nice to meet you too! Let's be friends! 🤝

💡 Teacher Tip: Swap roles! Then try with a real classmate using your own name and age.

🎤 Unit 5

Show & Tell Time!

Stand up tall, smile, and tell your friends about yourself! Use this checklist:

  • 1
    Say "Hello! My name is ___."
  • 2
    Say "I am ___ years old."
  • 3
    Say "My favorite color is ___."
  • 4
    Say "My favorite animal is ___."
  • 5
    Say "Thank you! 😊" and bow or wave!

🌟 Bonus: Bring a favorite toy or drawing to show your friends while you talk!

🍎 Teacher & Parent Corner

Tips for Success!

🪞

Practice in the Mirror

Kids love practicing their introduction in front of a mirror — it builds confidence!

🎭

Use Puppets

Let a puppet ask the questions first. Kids feel braver answering a puppet friend.

👏

Cheer Loudly

Clap and cheer after every try, even small ones — confidence grows with praise.

🔁

Repeat Daily

Say the introduction every morning at home or in class to build fluency naturally.
