🔗 Family Relationships (How We Are Connected)

Let's understand how family members are connected!

👨‍👩‍👧 What is a family?
A family is a group of people related by blood, marriage, or love. Families take care of each other and show love every day!
👩 + 👨 = 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family!
A mother and father together have children. These children are brothers and sisters to each other. This is a nuclear family!
👴 👵 = Grandparents
Grandparents are the parents of your mother or father. They are very special and tell us stories!
👫 = Cousins
Cousins are the children of your aunts and uncles. They are your relatives and friends!
