Listening Script

Audio Text — Read Aloud or Play Recording  ·  2 Scripts

🎙️ SCRIPT A — "Budi's Morning Routine"
Good morning, everyone! My name is Budi. 👋

Every morning, I wake up at six o'clock.
First, I go to the bathroom. I brush my teeth and take a bath.
Then, I get dressed. I wear my school uniform. 👕

After that, I eat breakfast with my family. 🍳
Today, I eat rice, egg, and vegetables. It is delicious!

At seven o'clock, I go to school. 🎒
I walk to school with my friend, Sari.

At school, I study English, Math, and Science.
I also play outside during break time. 🏃

At twelve o'clock, I eat lunch. I eat fried rice. 🍱

In the afternoon, I go home. I do my homework and study again.
I watch TV for one hour. 📺

At seven o'clock in the evening, I eat dinner with my family. 🍽️
Then I brush my teeth again and go to bed.
I sleep at nine o'clock. 😴

That is my daily life. Goodnight, everyone! 🌙
💡 Teacher's Tip Read the script expressively with clear intonation. Read it 2–3 times. First: students only listen. Second: students point to pictures of each activity as it's mentioned. Third: students repeat short sentences along with the teacher.
🎙️ SCRIPT B — "Sari's Day" (Short Version)
Hi! I am Sari. This is my day! 😊

In the morning: I wake up, brush my teeth, take a bath, and eat breakfast. 🌅

In the morning too: I go to school and study with my friends. 🎒📖

At noon: I eat lunch at school. I eat noodles today. 🍜

In the afternoon: I play outside with my friends. We play jump rope! 🏃

At night: I eat dinner, watch TV a little, then sleep. 🌙

I have a great day every day! Do you? 😄
💡 Teacher's Tip Script B is shorter and great for review sessions or students who need extra repetition. It can also be used as a simple dictation activity — ask students to draw each activity they hear.
📋 ACTIVITY — Put in Order!

After listening to Script A, put Budi's activities in the correct order from morning to night!

  • 1🌅 Wake up at 6 o'clock
  • 2🦷 Brush teeth & take a bath
  • 3🍳 Eat breakfast with family
  • 4🎒 Go to school at 7 o'clock
  • 5📖 Study at school
  • 6🍱 Eat lunch at 12 o'clock
  • 7📺 Watch TV at home
  • 8🍽️ Eat dinner with family
  • 9😴 Sleep at 9 o'clock
💡 Teacher's Tip Print or write each activity on separate cards and shuffle them. After listening to the script, students arrange the cards in the correct order. This builds sequencing skills while reinforcing listening comprehension.

Listening Dialogue

Everyday Conversations  ·  2 Dialogues

💬 DIALOGUE 1 — "Good Morning!"
👦
Budi: Good morning, Sari! How are you today?
👧
Sari: Good morning, Budi! I am very well, thank you! And you?
👦
Budi: I am great! Did you eat breakfast today?
👧
Sari: Yes! I ate bread and a glass of milk. Yummy! 🥛🍞
👦
Budi: Nice! I ate rice and egg. What time did you wake up?
👧
Sari: I woke up at six o'clock. I was not late! 😄
👦
Budi: Great! Let's go to school together! 🎒
👧
Sari: Yes! Let's go! Bye, Mom! 👋
💡 Teacher's Tip Read the dialogue with students — teacher as Budi, students as Sari, then swap roles. Focus on intonation when asking and answering questions. Practice the pronunciation of "woke up" and "o'clock."
💬 DIALOGUE 2 — "After School"
👩
Mom: Welcome home, Sari! How was school today?
👧
Sari: It was fun, Mom! I studied English and played with Budi! 😊
👩
Mom: That's wonderful! Are you hungry? Let's eat lunch.
👧
Sari: Yes, I am very hungry! What is for lunch? 🍽️
👩
Mom: We have fried rice and chicken soup! Your favorite! 🍲
👧
Sari: Yay! Thank you, Mom! You are the best! ❤️
💡 Teacher's Tip After reading the dialogue, do a role-play in pairs. Students can swap in their own favorite foods to personalize their lines. This builds creativity while keeping the sentence patterns intact.

Listening Exercises

Listening Practice  ·  3 Exercise Types

📝 EXERCISE A — Choose the Correct Answer

Listen to Budi's Morning Routine, then choose the correct answer! 👇

1. What time does Budi wake up?
2. What does Budi eat for breakfast?
3. What time does Budi go to school?
4. What does Budi eat for lunch?
5. What time does Budi sleep?
6. Who does Budi walk to school with?
💡 Teacher's Tip Re-read Script A before students answer. Give about 30 seconds per question. Review answers together — clap for every correct answer! 🎉
✅ EXERCISE B — True or False?

Circle TRUE or FALSE based on Budi and Sari's stories!

1. Budi brushes his teeth in the morning.
2. Budi goes to school by bus.
3. Budi eats lunch at twelve o'clock.
4. Budi watches TV for three hours.
5. Sari woke up at six o'clock.
6. Sari and her mom eat fried rice for lunch.

Listening Song

Daily Life Song  ·  Sing & Act!

🎶 "MY DAILY ROUTINE" — Sing Along!
🌅 I wake up in the morning,
I stretch and stop my yawning, 🥱
I brush my teeth so bright,
They shine like stars at night! ⭐

🛁 I take a bath, splash splash!
I get dressed very fast!
I eat my breakfast well,
I have so much to tell! 📣

🎒 I go to school at seven,
My classroom feels like heaven! 😇
I study, learn, and play,
I love each school day! 🏫

🍽️ I eat my lunch at noon,
The food is done so soon! 😋
I play outside and run,
Oh, afternoon is fun! 🏃

🌙 At night I eat my dinner,
Each day I get a winner! 🏆
I close my eyes and sleep,
Sweet dreams so soft and deep... 😴💤

♩ Sung to a cheerful 4/4 marching beat ♩
Add actions for each activity while singing!
🕺 SONG ACTIONS GUIDE

Do these actions while singing the song!

  • 🌅Wake up — Open eyes wide, raise both hands up
  • 🦷Brush teeth — Move hand left and right like brushing teeth
  • 🛁Take a bath — Rub left and right shoulder with opposite hands
  • 🍳Eat breakfast — Scoop hand toward mouth like eating
  • 🎒Go to school — March in place while pretending to carry a backpack
  • 📖Study — Open both palms flat like opening a book
  • 🏃Play outside — Run in place with a big smile
  • 😴Sleep — Place both palms together under cheek, close eyes
💡 Teacher's Tip Sing this song at the start or end of the lesson as a warm-up or cool-down. Repeat at least 3 times so students can memorize it. Award a star sticker to the most expressive student! ⭐ Teachers can also record the song for students to listen to as homework.
🗣️ SPEAKING CHALLENGE — Tell Your Routine!

After listening and singing, it's your turn to talk! Use these sentence starters:

  • 1I wake up at _____ o'clock.
  • 2I eat breakfast. I eat _____.
  • 3I go to school at _____ o'clock.
  • 4At school, I _____.
  • 5At night, I sleep at _____ o'clock.
💡 Teacher's Tip Ask each student to come to the front and share their own routine using the sentence starters above. This is the output of the full listening cycle: vocab → script → dialogue → exercise → song → speaking!
