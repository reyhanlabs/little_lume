❓ Unit 5

Let's Answer Questions!

Read "My Dog Dot" again, then answer these questions:

  • 1
    What does the story tell us about? (A dog, a cat, or a bird?)
  • 2
    What size is the dog — big or small?
  • 3
    What color is the dog?
  • 4
    Name two things the dog can do.
  • 5
    How does the person in the story feel about the dog?

🌟 Bonus: Can you draw a picture of Dot the dog?

🍎 Teacher & Parent Corner

Tips for Success!

🔁

Repeat Every Day

Just 10–15 minutes of reading practice each day builds habits that last a lifetime.

👏

Celebrate Effort

Praise the attempt, not just the answer. "Great try!" goes a long way for 5-year-olds.

☝️

Point as You Read

Use a finger to track each word left-to-right. This builds directionality and word recognition.

🎭

Make It Silly!

Use funny voices for characters. Kids who laugh while learning remember more — promise!
