Exercise F & G

Read the Text Carefully

Read the story below to answer the questions. (25 points)
🏕️ A Trip to Mount Bromo

Last school holiday, my family and I went to East Java to visit Mount Bromo. We left our house in the evening and travelled by train to Malang. We arrived at midnight and quickly rented a jeep to take us to the mountain.

It was freezing cold when we reached the viewpoint at 4:00 AM. I wore a thick jacket, gloves, and a beanie. We waited patiently for the sun to rise. When the sun finally came up, the view was absolutely breathtaking! The sky turned orange and pink, and we could see the beautiful crater of the mountain.

After enjoying the sunrise, we went down to the "Sea of Sand." We decided to ride horses to reach the stairs of the crater. It was my first time riding a horse, and I was a bit scared but very excited. We climbed 250 stairs to see the crater closely. It was tiring, but the experience was unforgettable. We took many pictures before returning to the hotel.

F. True (T) or False (F)? Select T or F based on the text. (10 pts)
1. The writer's family travelled to East Java by bus.  
2. They reached the viewpoint early in the morning before sunrise.  
3. The weather at the mountain was very hot.  
4. They rode horses at the "Sea of Sand".  
5. They climbed 250 stairs to reach the hotel.
