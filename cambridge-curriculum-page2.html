📚 Learning Objectives by Stage

Stages 1-4: Foundational to Intermediate Skills

1
Foundation (Age 5-6)
Learning to Read & Write
📖 Reading
  • Phonics & initial sounds
  • Simple word recognition
  • Read simple sentences
  • Understand main idea
  • Enjoy stories with adult
✏️ Writing & 🗣️ Speaking
  • Form letters correctly
  • Write labels & captions
  • Speak in simple sentences
  • Listen & follow instructions
  • Answer simple questions
2
Emergent (Age 6-7)
Early Competency Development
📖 Reading
  • Blend phonemes into words
  • Read labels & lists
  • Understand sequence
  • Recognize story patterns
  • Know parts of book
✏️ Writing & 🗣️ Speaking
  • Write simple sentences
  • Use basic punctuation
  • Spell common words
  • Share experiences verbally
  • Take turns in conversation
3
Developing (Age 7-8)
Comprehension & Composition Growth
📖 Reading
  • Read fluently with expression
  • Extract information
  • Understand cause & effect
  • Make simple predictions
  • Discuss character feelings
✏️ Writing & 🗣️ Speaking
  • Write narratives with sequence
  • Use expanded noun phrases
  • Use correct tenses
  • Speak confidently to groups
  • Ask & answer questions
4
Intermediate (Age 8-9)
Text Analysis & Extended Writing
📖 Reading
  • Make inferences & predictions
  • Understand author's purpose
  • Analyze character development
  • Identify text features
  • Compare different texts
✏️ Writing & 🗣️ Speaking
  • Write structured paragraphs
  • Use variety of connectives
  • Write for different purposes
  • Use presentation techniques
  • Participate in discussions
