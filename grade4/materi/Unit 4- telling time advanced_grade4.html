Grammar Lesson

It's... / past... / to... 🧩

Exact hour:

It's + hour + o'clock
It's three o'clock. 🕒 (3:00)

Half and quarter:

It's + half / quarter + past / to + hour
It's half past three. 🕞 (3:30)
It's quarter past three. 🕒 (3:15)
It's quarter to four. 🕜 (3:45)

Any number of minutes:

It's + minutes + past / to + hour
It's ten past three. 🕥 (3:10)
It's twenty to four. 🕓 (3:40)

The Golden Rule: minutes 1–30 → say "past" the same hour. Minutes 31–59 → say "to" the next hour!

Grammar Tips

Don't make these mistakes! ⚠️

✔ Always start with "It's". ✔ Minutes 1–30 use past. ✔ Minutes 31–59 use to the next hour.

❌ It's three and half.
✅ It's half past three.
❌ It's forty to four.
✅ It's twenty to four. (60 − 40 = 20)
❌ It's past ten three.
✅ It's ten past three.
❌ It's quarter to three fifteen.
✅ It's quarter past three. (3:15)

Summary

What we learned 🌟

  • Every time sentence starts with "It's".
  • Exact hour: It's + hour + o'clock.
  • Minutes 1–30: It's + minutes + past + hour (15 = quarter, 30 = half).
  • Minutes 31–59: It's + minutes + to + NEXT hour.
  • We can add in the morning / in the afternoon / in the evening / at night.
