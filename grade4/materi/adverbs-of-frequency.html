Grammar Lesson

Where does the adverb go? 🧩

Adverbs of frequency tell us how often something happens.

With action verbs:

Subject + Adverb + Verb
I always eat breakfast.
She usually walks to school.
They often play football.
We sometimes watch movies.

With the verb "be":

Subject + be + Adverb
I am always happy.
She is usually busy.
They are often late.
He is never angry.
Grammar Tips

Don't make these mistakes! ⚠️

✔ Adverbs come before action verbs.
✔ Adverbs come after "be" verbs.

❌ I eat always breakfast.
✅ I always eat breakfast.
❌ She always is happy.
✅ She is always happy.
❌ He plays never chess.
✅ He never plays chess.
