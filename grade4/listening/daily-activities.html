Morning Routine – Dani & Mom

👩 Mom – Female voice 👦 Dani – Boy voice
Press ▶️ to play
💡 Teacher's Note: Click ▶️ to play. Students listen WITHOUT reading the script first. Play 2–3 times for better understanding.
📜 Audio Script – Read After Listening

Mom Dani, wake up! It is already six o'clock in the morning!

Dani Oh, okay, Mom. Good morning!

Mom Good morning, dear. Please go and brush your teeth, then get dressed in your school uniform.

Dani Yes, Mom. Can I have breakfast first?

Mom Of course! I made fried rice and a glass of milk for you.

Dani Yay! My favourite! Mom, what time does the school bus come?

Mom It comes at six thirty. Do not forget your bag and your lunch box!

Dani Okay, Mom! Thank you. Bye bye!

Mom Bye bye, Dani! Have a great day at school!

Audio 2 – Monologue: My Daily Activities

My Daily Activities – Siti

👧 Siti – Female voice Clear & friendly
Press ▶️ to play
💡 Teacher's Note: Click ▶️ to play Siti's monologue. Students take notes on key details. Play 2–3 times.
📜 Audio Script – Read After Listening

Siti Hi! My name is Siti. I am a Grade 4 student. Let me tell you about my daily activities!

Every morning, I wake up at five thirty. First, I take a bath and brush my teeth. Then, I eat breakfast with my family. We usually have rice and vegetables.

I leave home at six fifteen. I walk to school because my school is very close to my house. School starts at seven o'clock.

At school, I study Mathematics, English, and Science, and other subjects. My favourite subject is English because it is very fun and useful!

I go home at one o'clock. After lunch, I take a short rest. In the afternoon, I do my homework. In the evening, I study and read story books.

At nine o'clock, it is bedtime. I go to sleep. Good night, everyone!

Top Listening Tips
🔄

Listen Multiple Times

It is okay to listen 2–3 times until you understand the message fully.

🗝️

Look for Keywords

Focus on important words like times, names, numbers, and places.

✍️

Take Notes

Write down words or numbers you hear while listening. Even short notes help!

😌

Don't Panic!

You do not need to understand every word. Just catch the main idea!
