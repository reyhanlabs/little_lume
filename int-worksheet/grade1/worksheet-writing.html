Choose the Correct Word

Circle the correct word in brackets. (10 points)
  • 1. My mother ( cook / cooks ) breakfast every morning.
  • 2. Yesterday, I ( go / went ) to the zoo with my family.
  • 3. The dog ( have / has ) big brown eyes.
  • 4. ( Do / Does ) you like ice cream?
  • 5. I studied hard, ( and / but / so ) I got a good score.
  • 6. She ( is / are ) my best friend.
  • 7. Last Sunday, we ( visit / visited ) Grandma.
  • 8. They ( don't / doesn't ) play outside today.
  • 9. ( Is / Are ) your teachers kind?
  • 10. Budi ( run / runs ) to school every day.

📄 My Pet Cat

I have a pet cat named Mochi. Mochi is a small, fluffy cat with orange and white fur. She has big green eyes and a long tail. Every morning, Mochi wakes me up by sitting on my face! I give her cat food and fresh water every day. She loves to play with a ball of string. Mochi is very playful but also very gentle. She is my favourite pet and my best friend.
