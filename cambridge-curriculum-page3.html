Stages 5-6, Text Types & Assessment Framework

Advanced Learning Objectives: Stages 5-6
5
Advanced (Age 9-10)
Critical Analysis & Composition
  • 📖 Reading
  • Analyze author's techniques
  • Understand implied meanings
  • Compare texts & authors
  • Evaluate plot structure
  • Identify themes & symbolism
  • ✏️ Writing & 🗣️ Speaking
  • Write extended narratives
  • Develop characters & settings
  • Use varied sentence types
  • Edit & improve drafts
6
Mastery (Age 10-11)
Advanced Analysis & Synthesis
  • 📖 Reading
  • Critically evaluate texts
  • Analyze stylistic features
  • Understand context & culture
  • Make sophisticated inferences
  • Analyze poetry in depth
  • ✏️ Writing & 🗣️ Speaking
  • Write for audience & purpose
  • Use persuasive techniques
  • Master complex punctuation
  • Develop personal style & voice
📖 Text Types & Genres

Stages 1-2: Picture books, simple stories, rhymes, fables | Labels, lists, instructions

Stages 3-4: Traditional tales, fantasy, adventure, poems | Recounts, reports, letters

Stages 5-6: Classics, extended narratives, poetry, playscripts | Biographies, arguments, journalism

✅ Assessment Framework
✓ Progression Tests (Stages 3-6) Teacher-marked with detailed performance data
✓ Checkpoint (Stage 6) End-of-primary with international benchmarks
✓ Formative Ongoing classroom observation & feedback
✓ Implementation 4-5 hrs/week (120-150 hrs/year)
