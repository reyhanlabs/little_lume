📚 Cambridge Primary English

Curriculum Framework (0058) - Overview & Foundation

About Cambridge Primary English: An internationally recognized curriculum for learners aged 5-11 years (Stages 1-6). Promotes enquiry-based learning, confidence, creativity, and intellectual engagement. Develops effective communicators who see themselves as readers and writers.
📖 The Three Core Learning Strands
📖
READING
Recognition • Interpretation • Vocabulary. Focus: Decoding, understanding, analyzing texts for meaning.
✏️
WRITING
Composition • Transcription • Handwriting. Focus: Writing for purpose, correct mechanics, creative expression.
🗣️
SPEAKING & LISTENING
Speaking • Listening • Interaction. Focus: Oral fluency, active listening, collaborative discussion.
🎯 Six Progressive Stages (Age 5-11)

✨ Key Curriculum Features
✓ Integrated Learning All lessons combine Reading, Writing & Speaking/Listening for holistic competence.
✓ Enquiry-Based Promotes critical thinking, creativity & intellectual engagement through investigation.
✓ Progressive Skills Systematic progression from Stage 1-6 with clear learning objectives.
✓ Diverse Texts Exposure to fiction, poetry, playscripts & non-fiction from multiple cultures.
