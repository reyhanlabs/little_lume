Audio 1 – Dialogue: Morning Routine

Morning Routine – Dani & Mom

🎵 Duration: approx. 1:30 min | Listen 2–3 times

Click ▶️ to listen
ℹ️ Teacher's note: Play the audio or read the dialogue below aloud. Students listen without reading first.
📜 Audio Script

Mom Dani, wake up! It's already 6 o'clock in the morning!

Dani Oh, okay, Mom. Good morning!

Mom Good morning, dear. Please go and brush your teeth, then get dressed in your uniform.

Dani Yes, Mom. Can I have breakfast first?

Mom Of course! I made fried rice and a glass of milk for you.

Dani Yay! My favourite! Mom, what time does the school bus come?

Mom At 6:30. Don't forget your bag and your lunch box!

Dani Okay, Mom! Thank you. Bye-bye!

Mom Bye-bye, Dani! Have a good day at school! 👋

Audio 2 – Monologue: My Daily Activities

My Daily Activities – Siti

🎵 Duration: approx. 1:15 min | Listen 2–3 times

Click ▶️ to listen
ℹ️ Teacher's note: Read this monologue slowly with clear intonation. Students write down key information.
📜 Audio Script

Hi! My name is Siti. I am a student. Let me tell you about my daily activities.

Every morning, I wake up at five thirty. I take a bath and brush my teeth. Then I eat breakfast with my family. We usually have rice and vegetables.

I go to school at six fifteen. I walk to school because my school is close to my house. School starts at seven o'clock.

At school, I study Math, English, Science, and other subjects. My favourite subject is English because it is fun!

I go home at one o'clock. After lunch, I take a rest. In the afternoon, I do my homework. In the evening, I study and read books.

At nine o'clock, I go to bed. Good night! 😴

Listening Tips
🔄

Listen Multiple Times

It's okay to listen to the audio 2–3 times until you understand it.

🗝️

Find Key Words

Focus on important words like times, names, and places.

✍️

Take Notes

Write down words or numbers you hear while listening.

😌

Don't Panic

You don't need to understand every word. Focus on the main idea!
