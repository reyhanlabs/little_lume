Audio 1 – Dialogue: Morning Time!

Morning Time! – Riko & Dad

🎵 Duration: approx. 1:15 min | Listen 2–3 times

Click ▶️ to listen
ℹ️ Teacher's note: Play the audio or read the dialogue aloud. Ask students to listen first without reading. Listen at least twice before doing the exercises.
📜 Audio Script

Dad Riko, wake up! It's six o'clock!

Riko Good morning, Dad! I'm sleepy...

Dad Come on! Go and wash your face. Then get ready for school.

Riko Okay, Dad. What is for breakfast today?

Dad We have bread and eggs! Your favourite!

Riko Yay! Can I have orange juice too, Dad?

Dad Of course! Now hurry up. The school starts at seven.

Riko Yes, Dad! I will get my bag and my water bottle.

Dad Good boy! Have a great day at school, Riko! 😊

Riko Thank you, Dad! Bye-bye! 👋

Audio 2 – Monologue: My Busy Day!

My Busy Day! – Nana

🎵 Duration: approx. 1:10 min | Listen 2–3 times

Click ▶️ to listen
ℹ️ Teacher's note: Read this monologue slowly with clear intonation. Students can write down key words or numbers they hear before answering.
📜 Audio Script

Hello! My name is Nana. I am in Grade One. Let me tell you about my day!

Every morning, I wake up at five o'clock. First, I wash my face and brush my teeth. Then I eat breakfast. I like to eat bread with jam in the morning.

I go to school at six thirty. My mom takes me to school by motorcycle. At school, I study and play with my friends. My favourite subject is English!

I go home at twelve o'clock. When I get home, I take a bath and eat lunch. Then I take a nap.

In the afternoon, I play with my little brother. At night, I do my homework and read a story book. I go to sleep at eight thirty.

That is my day! What about yours? 😊

Listening Tips
🔄

Listen Again & Again

It's okay to listen 2–3 times. Every listen helps you understand more!

🕐

Listen for Numbers

Time words like "six o'clock" or "twelve" are very important clues.

✍️

Write Key Words

Write down words or numbers you hear to help you remember.

😌

Stay Calm

You don't need every word. Focus on the main idea and key details!
